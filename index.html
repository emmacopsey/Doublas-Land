Visit Pondston

The homeland of the famous Doublas the frog

Top three activities to do at Pondston

Swimming

What better way to experience Doublas' mythical childhood than swimming in Pondston's legendary Rostapos pond?

Croaking

Learn the traditional croaks of Doublas' home clan, the Brostons. Become a master of the creaky croak.

Sleeping

After a long day, have a rest at one of the pondside villas modelled after Doublas' own childhood home.

Your guide

Drinkar the Third

Drinkar, the only known decendant of Doublas, at your service.
